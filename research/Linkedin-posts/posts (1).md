## Patterns Observed

1. **B2B-specific always** — never generic "entrepreneur" content, always B2B marketing
2. **Real numbers included** — metrics from Drift, Exit Five, and actual campaigns
3. **Podcast clips repurposed** — video snippets appear 1–2x per week
4. **Strong point of view** — always has a clear opinion, never neutral "here are some tips"
5. **Community call-outs** — regularly references Exit Five members, building group identity
6. **Soft promotion woven in naturally** — never hard selling, always value first
