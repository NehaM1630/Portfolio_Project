## Patterns Observed

1. **Personal photos in every post** — brand recognition through consistent visuals
2. **POV hooks** — "POV:" framing stops scroll effectively
3. **Story-first, lesson-second** — emotional engagement before tactical advice
4. **Short sentences, lots of line breaks** — maximizes readability especially on mobile
5. **High comment volume** — often 800–1,000+ comments, far above platform average
6. **Replies to almost every comment** — comments feed the algorithm AND build loyal community
