### Post 4 — The Content Hierarchy for B2B SaaS
**Date:** December 2025 | **Format:** Text post | **Likes:** ~690 | **Comments:** ~80

**Summary:** Tier 1: Case studies (high effort, high impact — quarterly). Tier 2: Frameworks and playbooks (medium effort, high impact — monthly). Tier 3: How-to blog posts (medium effort, medium impact). Tier 4: Social posts (low effort, variable impact — 3–5x/week). Key insight: most companies start with Tier 4 and never build Tier 1 or 2, so their LinkedIn content has no substance beneath it.
