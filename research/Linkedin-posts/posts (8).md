### Post 1 — BOFU Content That Actually Converts
**Date:** March 2026 | **Format:** Text post | **Likes:** ~520 | **Comments:** ~65

**Summary:** BOFU = Bottom of Funnel. Most B2B SaaS content is TOFU (awareness blogs, thought leadership) that generates traffic but doesn't convert. The 3 types of BOFU content that do convert: comparison pages ("X vs. Competitor — Honest Breakdown"), use-case landing pages ("How [job title] uses [product] to [outcome]"), and ROI calculators. These convert 3x better than blog posts because they target buyers who are already actively deciding.
