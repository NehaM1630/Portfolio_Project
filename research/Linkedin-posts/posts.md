## Patterns Observed

1. **Short lines, maximum white space** — never more than 2 sentences before a line break
2. **Hook is always a tension or contrast** — "X vs. Y" or "what everyone does vs. what works"
3. **No external links** — full value delivered natively, always
4. **Post length: 150–300 words** — long enough to teach, short enough to read in 60 seconds
5. **Always replies to comments** — builds algorithm signal and community
6. **Story + Lesson formula** — personal experience followed by one transferable insight
